## Content and media lanes

- Faceless content channels: local scripts, thumbnails, metadata, and batch plans first; no posting without human approval.
- Viewmax video workflow: research viewmax.io and viewmax.studio separately before any purchase.
- Higgsfield/Open Higgsfield content generation: compare official Higgsfield with cheaper/open alternatives if costs or limits block content production.
- Picsart-style flow alternatives: evaluate whether image-to-video or template-based tools can create faster content assets.
- VidRush AI or build similar: evaluate as short-form repurposing or "build our own" workflow.

## Review-before-sell rule

Ghoti may create:

- product idea cards
- offer stacks
- build packs
- listing drafts
- content batches
- launch checklists
- metrics intake templates

Ghoti must not:

- publish listings
- take payments
- upload products
- create accounts
- post content
- send outreach
- run ads
- claim results without proof

The operator must review and manually approve every public or money-facing action.

## Paid-tool ROI approval rule

Every paid tool or spend recommendation must include:

- expected use case
- estimated cost
- ROI or learning hypothesis
- spending cap
- stop-loss/cancel condition
- review checkpoint
- free/local alternative

Examples:

- Viewmax, Higgsfield, Arcads, Kling, and Seedance may be worth paying for only if the content pipeline has a clear offer, cadence, and measurement plan.
- OpenRouter/API spend must be capped, logged, and tied to a specific experiment.
- Ads spend should wait until there is a validated offer and manual approval.
- App-store fees should wait until a product is reviewed and approved.

## Safety and compliance gates

- No fake proof or fake testimonials.
- No fake scarcity.
- No spam or fake engagement.
- No child-targeted deceptive content.
- No content using copyrighted assets without rights.
- No financial, medical, legal, or employment guarantees.
- Platform compliance review is required before YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Whop, Gumroad, app-store, or ad platform use.

## Weekly review connection

Every product/content idea should eventually connect to:

- experiment tracker
- content shot tracker
- product draft tracker
- metrics intake
- weekly money review
- manual decision queue

This keeps the numbers-game system measurable instead of turning into a pile of disconnected ideas.
