# Codex N+3.20 Distribution and Email List Review Plan

Status: codex_planning_only / distribution_review_spec / approval_gated
Date: 2026-04-29

## Purpose

The weekly money review should make distribution and email-list gaps painfully visible. Ghoti should not let the operator create endless products with no exposure.

## Distribution Gap Detection

For each experiment, weekly review should check:

- Does it have at least 3 distribution channels?
- Does it include one short-form/social discovery channel?
- Does it include one owned-audience/email-list path?
- Does it include one marketplace, community, SEO, or long-form path where relevant?
- Is there a clear CTA?
- Is there a validation metric?
- Is approval required before public action?

Flag as a gap when:

- `distribution_channels` is missing or empty.
- only future account-dependent channels exist.
- no email-list/lead magnet angle exists.
- no content repurposing path exists.
- no next action exists.
- the next action is vague.

## Email List Opportunities

The review should generate draft-only opportunities such as:

- free checklist.
- free prompt sample.
- one-page setup guide.
- mini case-study breakdown.
- "watch me build this" build-log signup.
- Notion/Obsidian template preview.
- content batch swipe file.

For each opportunity, include:

- lead magnet title.
- target audience.
- reason it fits the experiment.
- one opt-in CTA.
- three-email welcome sequence idea.
- approval gate before account creation or email sending.

## Social Media Exposure Strategy

The weekly review should propose draft-only social angles:

- TikTok/Reels/Shorts hook.
- X/Twitter thread.
- LinkedIn carousel if professional audience fits.
- Reddit/community discussion only where rules allow.
- YouTube long-form or build-log angle if the topic needs depth.

Every suggestion must include:

- platform.
- hook.
- CTA.
- repurposing option.
- approval requirement.

## Repurposing Strategy

One source should become many artifacts:

| Source | Repurpose To |
| --- | --- |
| video-to-money review | product card, 5 hooks, email topic, tracker record |
| product idea | short-form script, landing page outline, lead magnet |
| content batch | email newsletter, thread, carousel, short video |
| weekly review | next actions, shot backlog, distribution checklist |

The review should recommend repurposing before creating unrelated new work.

## Anti-Spam And Anti-Fake-Engagement Rules

The weekly review must forbid:

- purchased followers.
- bot views.
- comment pods.
- fake reviews.
- fake testimonials.
- fake scarcity.
- fake income proof.
- scraped email lists.
- unsolicited mass DMs.
- automated follow/unfollow.
- platform TOS bypass.

## Approval Gates

Approval is required before:

- creating or logging into social accounts.
- posting content.
- scheduling posts.
- sending email.
- creating an email list.
- creating a product listing.
- setting a price publicly.
- paid ads.
- affiliate/referral setup.
- website/domain/hosting purchase.
- app-store or marketplace submission.

## Weekly Review Output Expectations

The `distribution_gaps.md` artifact should include:

- experiments with fewer than 3 channels.
- missing email-list angle.
- missing CTA.
- missing repurposing path.
- missing approval status.

The `email_list_opportunities.md` artifact should include:

- top 3 lead magnet ideas.
- top 3 opt-in CTAs.
- top 3 email sequence concepts.
- all marked draft-only until approval.

## Strategic Truth

Exposure is not optional. The weekly review should reinforce that every serious shot needs a distribution plan, an email-list angle, and a measurement plan before it deserves more build time.
