## Purpose

Review how distribution and exposure should be built into every money experiment. Ghoti should not only generate products; it should generate paths to attention, feedback, opt-ins, and sales while preserving approval gates.

## Core Rule

Every experiment needs at least 3 distribution channels before it is considered ready for review.

Example channel mix:

- short-form platform
- email list / lead magnet
- community or search channel

No channel is used live until the operator approves.

## Required Distribution Fields

Each experiment candidate should include:

- primary channel
- secondary channel
- tertiary channel
- email list angle
- short-form angle
- CTA
- repurposing plan
- validation metric
- shot counter
- weekly review date

## Email List Angle

Each experiment should answer:

- What free thing earns an opt-in?
- What problem does the lead magnet solve?
- What 3-email follow-up would make sense later?
- What product or offer does the lead magnet naturally lead to?

Email actions remain blocked:

- no email service provider setup without approval
- no list import
- no purchased list
- no scraped list
- no automated sends

## Short-Form Angle

Each experiment should produce at least:

- 5 hooks
- 3 short scripts
- 1 CTA
- 1 repurposing note

Target platforms:

- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

Posting remains blocked until the user approves the exact content and platform.

## CTA Requirements

Every CTA should be honest and low-pressure:

- download a free checklist
- join an email list
- read the full guide
- see the template
- reply with a question
- vote on the next product

Forbidden CTAs:

- fake urgency
- fake scarcity
- misleading income claims
- "guaranteed results"
- engagement bait that violates platform rules
- automated comment/DM funnels without approval

## Repurposing Plan

Each source should be repurposed into:

- 1 product idea card
- 1 lead magnet idea
- 5 to 10 short-form hooks
- 1 long-form outline
- 1 email draft idea
- 1 community discussion prompt

This is how the numbers game compounds without burning the user out.

## Validation Metrics

Pick one primary metric per experiment:

- impressions
- clicks
- opt-ins
- replies
- sales
- watch time
- save rate
- email reply rate
- product page visits

Do not over-measure early. One clear metric is better than twenty empty metrics.

## Shot Counter

The system should track:

- total ideas generated
- total candidates scored
- total assets drafted
- total items approved
- total items published manually
- total items killed
- total winners scaled

Suggested weekly summary:

```text
Week YYYY-WW
ideas_generated: 30
experiments_scored: 10
assets_drafted: 5
approved_for_publish: 2
published_manual: 1
killed: 4
scaled: 0
top_learning: ""
```

## Weekly Review System

Weekly review should ask:

1. Which shots got the most signal?
2. Which shots were easiest to produce?
3. Which shots created email-list potential?
4. Which shots felt energizing for the operator?
5. Which shots were risky or unclear?
6. What gets scaled next week?
7. What gets killed?

## Channel-Specific Safety

TikTok / Instagram / YouTube Shorts:

- no reposting without rights
- no fake engagement
- no botting
- no misleading claims

X/Twitter / LinkedIn:

- no spam replies
- no engagement pods
- no fake case studies

Reddit / communities:

- obey community rules
- no link drops where disallowed
- no fake identity

Email:

- opt-in only
- unsubscribe compliance
- no scraped or purchased lists

Whop / marketplaces:

- no listing until product and claims are reviewed
- no fake reviews
- no inflated screenshots

## Recommendation

N+3.18 runner outputs should include distribution sections by default. N+3.19 should add a dashboard read view and shot counter so the operator can see whether the numbers game is actually happening.
