## Why Exposure Matters

Products do not sell if nobody sees them. Ghoti should help the user create more ethical exposure by turning ideas into many content pieces, lead magnets, offers, and distribution experiments. Exposure should be broad, consistent, and measured, but never fake, spammy, deceptive, or automated through unapproved live accounts.

## How To Build Audience Legally

- Use owned accounts only.
- Publish original or properly licensed content.
- Add clear disclosures for affiliate links and sponsored claims.
- Offer real value before asking for a sale.
- Respect platform rules and community norms.
- Keep outreach manual and review-first.
- Track what works without invasive scraping.
- Avoid mass automation until a separate legal/TOS review approves a narrow workflow.

## Why Email List Matters

An email list is an owned distribution asset. Platforms can throttle or ban content, but a consent-based email list preserves a direct relationship with people who asked to hear more.

Email list rules:

- opt-in only
- no purchased lists
- no scraped emails
- unsubscribe required
- no misleading subject lines
- no financial/medical/legal guarantees
- clear sender identity

## Daily Cadence

Minimum viable daily actions:

- capture 3 ideas
- draft 1 short-form hook
- improve 1 product or lead magnet asset
- record 1 metric from any published asset
- decide 1 next action

No posting happens automatically. The user approves before publishing.

## Weekly Cadence

- 20 to 50 raw ideas captured.
- 10 content drafts.
- 3 product or lead magnet drafts.
- 3 selected experiments.
- 1 to 3 approved posts or assets published manually.
- Metrics reviewed once per week.
- Kill or iterate weak shots.

## Anti-Spam Rules

- No fake engagement.
- No botting.
- No fake accounts.
- No mass unsolicited messaging.
- No scraped contact lists.
- No deceptive claims.
- No medical, financial, or legal guarantees.
- No harassment or targeting private individuals.
- No platform cap bypass.
- No reposting without rights.

## Owned-Account-Only Rules

- Use only accounts the user controls.
- Do not share credentials with unreviewed tools.
- Do not let Paperclip/OpenClaw/n8n/CUA touch accounts until separate approval.
- Keep draft and publish steps separate.
- Record the human approval that authorized each publish/send/listing action.
