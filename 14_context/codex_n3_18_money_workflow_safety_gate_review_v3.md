# Codex N+3.18 Money Workflow Safety Gate Review v3

Status: codex_static_audit_only / safety_gate_review / approval_gated

## Safety Truth

The N+3.18 direction is useful only if it stays as a local, artifact-only planning workflow. It should transform local notes or transcript-like text into ideas and experiment candidates, not perform the money workflow itself.

Allowed in N+3.18:

- Local `.md` or `.txt` input.
- Local Gemma/Ollama generation if already installed and approved for local brain use.
- Artifact-only outputs.
- Product ideas.
- Content angles.
- Distribution plans.
- Risk reviews.
- Experiment candidates.
- Experiment scoring.
- Human-reviewed next-action suggestions.

Forbidden in N+3.18:

- Fetching YouTube URLs directly.
- Downloading videos or transcripts.
- Scraping third-party platforms.
- Live posting.
- Outreach.
- Email sending.
- Selling or publishing products.
- Payment, trade, or money movement.
- App-store submission or account action.
- Live account login or automation.
- Browser/CUA action.
- Fake proof, fake engagement, fake testimonials, spam, or deceptive claims.
- Installing or wiring Paperclip, OpenClaw, n8n, Unity-MCP, Mythos, Dolphin, CUDA, Manus, or other external runtimes.

## Human Approval Gates

Human approval is required before:

- Any public post.
- Any email send.
- Any outreach.
- Any product listing.
- Any Whop, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Shopify, app-store, or marketplace action.
- Any account creation or login.
- Any paid tool use.
- Any scraping or third-party data collection.
- Any legal, tax, financial, investment, medical, or regulated claim.
- Any claim about earnings, results, or proof.

## Distribution And Exposure Scope

Distribution/exposure in N+3.18 should be planning/template-only.

Safe outputs:

- "Try TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and email list as candidate channels."
- "Draft three hooks for human review."
- "Create an email-list angle."
- "Mark posting approval as required."

Unsafe outputs:

- Automatically posting.
- Automatically sending email.
- Automatically collecting leads.
- Automatically contacting businesses.
- Auto-filling product listings.
- Using fake proof or fake reviews.
- Botting, spam, fake engagement, or deceptive scarcity.

## Numbers-Game Money OS Fit

This milestone supports the numbers-game money OS safely by turning one local source file into multiple possible shots:

- Product shots.
- Content shots.
- Distribution shots.
- Email-list lead magnet angles.
- Experiment candidates.
- Risk notes.
- Scoring inputs.

The value is volume plus structure, not autonomous action. Ghoti should help the operator create many safe, reviewable drafts and then choose which to publish manually.

## Gemma / Codex / Claude Split

- Gemma/Ollama: easy local summarization, idea extraction, content angle drafts, risk-label first pass.
- Codex: audits, static review, independent planning, safety checks.
- Claude Code: hard implementation, smoke tests, state updates, final validation, commit/push.

## Safety Verdict

The N+3.18 direction is safe if it remains local-only, artifact-only, and approval-gated. It becomes unsafe if it starts fetching URLs, scraping, posting, emailing, selling, logging into accounts, or executing model output.
