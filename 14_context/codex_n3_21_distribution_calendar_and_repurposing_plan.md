# Codex N+3.21 Distribution Calendar And Repurposing Plan

Status: codex_planning_only / distribution_calendar / not_runtime_wired

Date: 2026-04-29
Branch: feat/ghoti-visible-operator-stack

## Purpose

The content batch planner should not stop at ideas. It should convert each offer into a practical distribution calendar and repurposing map so the user gets more exposure without spam, fake engagement, or live automation.

## Distribution Principles

- Every experiment should have at least three possible distribution channels.
- Every product should have an email-list or owned-audience angle.
- Every content batch should include short-form, long-form, community, and email variants.
- Every public action requires human approval.
- Publishing and platform use are separate from artifact generation.

## Suggested Weekly Cadence

| Day | Drafting Focus | Output | Live Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Monday | Pick product or experiment. | One content brief and safety checklist. | None. |
| Tuesday | Generate batch. | 30 hooks, 10 long-form ideas, 10 email ideas. | None. |
| Wednesday | Select best shots. | Top 10 content shots and weekly calendar. | None unless approved. |
| Thursday | Polish assets. | Scripts, captions, email draft, CTA variants. | None unless approved. |
| Friday | Manual review. | Approval checklist and metric plan. | User may approve posting manually. |
| Weekend | Measure and learn. | Metrics update and kill/iterate decision. | Manual only. |

## Channel Routing

| Channel | Best Use | Draft Artifact | Safety Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| TikTok | Fast hook testing. | 15 to 30 second scripts. | No fake engagement or reposting without rights. |
| Instagram Reels | Visual credibility and short tips. | Reel scripts and caption drafts. | Owned account only. |
| YouTube Shorts | Search and discovery. | Shorts scripts and titles. | Respect reused content rules. |
| YouTube long-form | Authority and tutorials. | Outline, title, thumbnail concept. | No deceptive income claims. |
| X/Twitter | Build-in-public and threads. | Thread outline and single-post drafts. | No spam replies or engagement pods. |
| LinkedIn | Professional credibility. | Practical build-log posts. | Avoid exaggerated authority claims. |
| Reddit/community | Helpful participation. | Non-promotional helpful posts. | Follow community rules. |
| Email list | Owned audience. | Lead magnet and welcome email drafts. | Opt-in only; no purchased or scraped lists. |
| Whop/store later | Product marketplace. | Listing draft and FAQ. | No listing until approved. |

## Repurposing Ladder

One product idea should become:

1. One positioning statement.
2. One lead magnet idea.
3. Thirty short-form hooks.
4. Ten short scripts.
5. Five CTA variants.
6. Three long-form outlines.
7. Three email drafts.
8. Three community posts.
9. One landing-page outline.
10. One experiment tracker update after metrics exist.

## Email List Layer

Every batch should answer:

- What free thing would make the target customer opt in?
- What problem does the first email solve?
- What product does the list naturally lead toward?
- What promise can be made honestly?
- What proof is missing?

The planner should draft email ideas only. It should not create lists, send campaigns, or connect email tools.

## Metrics To Track

- impressions
- views
- clicks
- opt-ins
- replies
- sales
- watch time where available
- content creation time
- user energy or interest
- qualitative comments

## Anti-Spam And Claims Rules

- No fake engagement.
- No bots.
- No purchased views, likes, comments, followers, or email lists.
- No scraped outreach lists.
- No deceptive scarcity.
- No fake testimonials.
- No unverified income claims.
- No medical, legal, or financial guarantees.
- No live account action without explicit approval.

## Future Dashboard Fit

A later dashboard can show:

- shots drafted this week
- shots approved by user
- shots posted manually
- channels used
- email-list angle present yes/no
- content batches missing CTA
- experiments with no distribution plan

## Verdict

The content calendar should stay draft-only at N+3.21. It makes the money OS practical by turning products into exposure plans, while preserving a hard boundary between planning and publishing.
