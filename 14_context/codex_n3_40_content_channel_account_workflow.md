## Many Channels And Experiments

Numbers-game principle:

- Many channel ideas.
- Many content batches.
- Many short-form hooks.
- Many metadata/thumbnail tests.
- Many distribution angles.
- Honest metrics intake.
- Kill weak channels fast.
- Double down on channels with real audience response.

Volume must not become spam, fake engagement, or duplicate low-quality posting.

## Local Drafting Allowed

Allowed after future implementation approval:

- Niche brainstorming.
- Style guide drafting.
- Script drafting.
- Thumbnail concepts.
- Metadata/SEO drafts.
- Posting calendar drafts.
- Content batch generation.
- Local video generation/editing after tool approval.
- Metrics intake templates.

Not allowed without explicit approval:

- Creating accounts.
- Logging into accounts.
- Posting videos.
- Scheduling posts.
- Sending DMs/comments.
- Running ads.
- Buying engagement.
- Using stealth browser automation.
- Creating fake personas to deceive users.

## Human Approval Checkpoint

Before any public post, the operator must review:

- platform
- account identity
- video/script
- title
- description
- hashtags
- thumbnail
- CTA
- claims/proof
- copyright/asset rights
- child/kid-adjacent safety
- monetization/disclosure
- platform compliance

Approval phrase idea:

```text
APPROVE MANUAL CONTENT POST REVIEW FOR <channel_id>/<content_id>
```

This approval does not authorize Ghoti to post automatically. It only records that the operator reviewed the content pack and may manually publish externally.

## Platform Compliance Gates

Source-check findings support strict platform gates:

- YouTube prohibits fake engagement and artificial views/likes/comments.
- TikTok integrity rules call out bulk automation, fake personas, high-volume commercial abuse, and artificial engagement signals.
- FTC guidance around contests and endorsements supports clear disclosures, legitimate rules, and no misleading sweepstakes behavior.

## Child Or Kid-Adjacent Content

If a channel targets children or kid-adjacent audiences:

- Extra safety review is required.
- Avoid deceptive hooks.
- Avoid manipulative monetization.
- Avoid collecting child data.
- Review platform child-safety and advertising rules before publishing.
- Avoid AI-generated characters or voices that mislead children about identity or relationships.

## Trend Testing

Algorithms change. Use current research and controlled tests:

- Track hypothesis.
- Track post date/time.
- Track hook type.
- Track watch time.
- Track views.
- Track CTR if available.
- Track follows/subscribers.
- Track opt-ins.
- Track qualitative comments.

No dashboard or model should hallucinate algorithm rules. Use observed metrics and current platform guidance.

## Current Verdict

Content channel management is high priority. The safe next step is local channel briefs, style guides, content batches, and metrics intake. Account creation and posting remain manual, explicit, and approval-gated.
