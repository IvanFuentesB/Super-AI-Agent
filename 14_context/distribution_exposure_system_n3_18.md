## What This Is

A planning framework for distribution and exposure generated by the video-to-money runner as local draft artifacts. Not a live distribution system.

## Channels Covered in Runner Output

The `video_to_money` runner generates a `distribution_plan.md` artifact covering:

- Up to 3 named distribution channels
- Strategy notes per channel
- Email list angle (opt-in funnel)
- Short-form content angle (TikTok/Reels hook)
- Repurposing angle (how content can be reused)
- Validation metric (how to measure if distribution is working)

## The Numbers Game Principle

From the sample source notes (N+3.18 design intent):

- Distribution is the actual product, not the thing you built
- One product × 50 distribution attempts beats 50 products × 1 attempt each
- Email list is the only asset you own — social platforms can remove you
- Short-form (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) is the discovery layer; email is the conversion layer

These principles inform the runner's prompt structure but are not validated business claims.

## Funnel Sketch (Planning Only)

```
Short-form content (discovery) →
  Free opt-in lead magnet (email capture) →
    Email sequence (conversion) →
      Paid digital product (revenue)
```

This funnel is a planning model only. No step is automated. Every step requires human execution and approval.

## Safety Rules for Distribution

1. No fake testimonials or income claims in any content
2. No scraping of others' content without transformation
3. Email marketing must comply with CAN-SPAM/GDPR (opt-in only, easy unsubscribe)
4. No live account login in this runner
5. No payment processing in this runner
6. All content must be created manually by the operator after reviewing draft artifacts

## Relationship to Experiment Tracker

When an operator decides to move a distribution experiment forward, they:

1. Review the `distribution_plan.md` and `experiment_candidates.jsonl` artifacts
2. Manually record the experiment with `money_workflow_new_experiment.py` (with or without scoring)
3. Update `status` field in the tracker as distribution milestones are reached
4. Record observed metrics only — no invented or projected metrics
