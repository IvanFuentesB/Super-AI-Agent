## Purpose

Document the planned Whop workflows for Ghoti's money OS. All workflows are planning artifacts only. No Whop account creation, product uploads, pricing, or payment setup will occur without explicit operator approval.

---

## What Whop Is

Whop is a platform for selling digital products, communities, and subscriptions. It supports:
- One-time digital product sales
- Recurring subscriptions and communities
- Course/video hosting
- Affiliate programs
- Discord integrations

Whop is a candidate distribution platform for digital products, prompt packs, community access, and clipping workflows.

---

## Workflow 1: Digital Product Upload

**Goal:** Sell a completed digital product (prompt pack, template bundle, checklist) on Whop.

Steps (planning only — each requires approval before action):
1. Build and review the product locally (no account action needed)
2. Get operator approval: pricing, product description, store listing copy
3. Create Whop account (approval required — one-time)
4. Create product listing: title, description, price, deliverable
5. Upload product files
6. Set price and payout method (payment setup requires approval)
7. Publish listing (approval required)
8. Share link via approved channels only

Approval gates:
- [ ] Account creation approved
- [ ] Pricing approved
- [ ] Listing copy approved
- [ ] Product files reviewed
- [ ] Payment/payout method approved
- [ ] Publication approved

---

## Workflow 2: Prompt Pack Workflow

**Goal:** Bundle AI prompts into a paid Whop product.

Steps:
1. Generate prompt pack locally (Gemma can draft initial prompts as artifacts)
2. Review and edit prompts manually
3. Format as markdown, Notion export, or PDF
4. Write product page copy (Gemma can draft; operator reviews)
5. Follow Digital Product Upload workflow above (all gates apply)

Key notes:
- Prompt packs are low-effort, high-leverage digital products
- $9–$47 price range is typical for solo creator bundles
- Include a free sample (lead magnet) to reduce cold-sale friction
- No copy-paste of other creators' prompts without attribution/permission

---

## Workflow 3: Clipping Workflow

**Goal:** Clip or repurpose existing long-form content (operator's own content only) into short-form clips for distribution, then funnel to Whop community or product.

Steps:
1. Identify long-form video or content to clip (operator's own content only)
2. Extract clip timestamps or rough cuts (Gemma can draft from transcript)
3. Manual editing in video editor (no automated posting)
4. Review and approve clips before publishing
5. Add CTA directing to Whop community or product link

Approval gates:
- [ ] Content ownership confirmed (operator's own content only)
- [ ] Clips reviewed before any publish
- [ ] Whop link approved before including in CTA

---

## Workflow 4: Community Workflow

**Goal:** Create a paid Whop community around a niche (AI ops, productivity, content creation, etc.).

Steps:
1. Define community topic, positioning, and price point
2. Write welcome message, community rules, and onboarding flow
3. Get operator approval on all copy and pricing
4. Create Whop community (account + payment setup required)
5. Invite first members through organic/approved channels only

Approval gates:
- [ ] Community name and positioning approved
- [ ] Pricing approved
- [ ] Payment/payout approved
- [ ] Posting / inviting approved
