# Distribution and Exposure Checklist

Date: 2026-04-28
Milestone: N+3.17
Status: template_only / no_live_actions

---

## Core Principle

Exposure is the bottleneck, not ideas.

The best product with no distribution earns nothing. The goal is to build multiple compounding exposure channels over time, starting with the cheapest and most durable: owned channels (email list, long-form content) and free reach channels (short-form video, SEO).

---

## Email List

The most durable owned channel. Build this first.

- [ ] Choose an ESP (e.g. ConvertKit, Beehiiv, MailerLite) — account creation requires approval
- [ ] Create one lead magnet (free guide, checklist, or mini-tool) to drive opt-ins
- [ ] Set up a welcome email sequence (minimum 3 emails: welcome → value → offer)
- [ ] Add opt-in CTA to every piece of content
- [ ] Send at minimum 1 email per week to keep list warm
- [ ] No purchased lists, no scraped lists, no unsolicited adds
- [ ] CAN-SPAM / GDPR compliance reviewed before any sends

Approval required before: account creation, list imports, any email send.

---

## Short-Form Video

Highest-reach discovery channel at zero cost.

- [ ] Choose primary platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts)
- [ ] Post minimum 3–5 short-form videos per week for traction
- [ ] First 3 seconds must hook — test multiple hooks per topic
- [ ] Use a consistent CTA: "Link in bio" or "Comment X"
- [ ] No purchased views, fake followers, or engagement bots
- [ ] No TOS-violating automation (comment pods, follow-unfollow)
- [ ] Platform account creation requires approval
- [ ] Posting requires operator review and approval

Approval required before: account creation, first post, any automation.

---

## SEO and AI SEO

Compounding organic reach over weeks and months.

- [ ] Identify 10–20 low-competition keywords in the niche
- [ ] Create one written or video piece per keyword
- [ ] Optimize titles, descriptions, and metadata for search
- [ ] Consider AI-answer-engine optimization (answer common questions clearly)
- [ ] No keyword stuffing or black-hat SEO
- [ ] No AI-generated spam articles (quality and helpfulness first)

Approval required before: any website or blog creation, domain purchase, hosting setup.

## Content Repurposing

One piece of work → many distribution lanes.

| Source | Repurpose to | Platforms |
|---|---|---|
| Short-form video (60s) | Twitter/X thread or LinkedIn post | Twitter, LinkedIn |
| Long-form video | 5–10 short clips | TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts |
| Email newsletter | Blog post | Website / Medium |
| Carousel (10 slides) | Short-form hook video | TikTok, Instagram |
| Product demo | Tutorial short | YouTube Shorts |
| Podcast or voice note | Gemma summary → bullet article | Blog / newsletter |

## Community Distribution

- [ ] Identify 3–5 relevant communities (Reddit, Discord, Slack, Skool, Whop)
- [ ] Participate genuinely before promoting anything
- [ ] No spam, no link drops, no self-promotion without community rules check
- [ ] Affiliate/referral links require disclosure and platform TOS review
- [ ] Community account creation requires approval

---

## Legal and TOS Checks

- [ ] No fake engagement, purchased views, or bots on any platform
- [ ] No scraping of platform data beyond what is explicitly allowed by TOS
- [ ] No use of other people's content without permission or proper attribution
- [ ] No income claims that cannot be substantiated
- [ ] No affiliate links without clear disclosure
- [ ] No automated DMs, follow-unfollow, or comment automation
- [ ] Privacy policy and cookie compliance if any website collects data
- [ ] GDPR/CAN-SPAM compliance for email list
