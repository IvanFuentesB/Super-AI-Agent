# Codex N+3.27 Numbers-Game Operating Cadence

## Purpose

Define the weekly operating rhythm for Ghoti's money system. The goal is to create many ethical shots, measure honestly, expose useful work to real audiences with human approval, and compound what works.

## Core Principle

Winning is a numbers game:

- more shots
- more products
- more content
- more distribution attempts
- faster feedback
- honest metrics
- kill weak work quickly
- double down on real traction

The system should reward output and learning, not fantasy projections.

## Weekly Cadence

### 1. Generate Shots

Inputs:

- user ideas
- local notes
- money-making video transcripts or notes
- competitor observations
- customer pain points
- old course notes
- social trend ideas
- content batch artifacts
- manual launch metrics

Gemma can draft:

- product ideas
- content angles
- lead magnet ideas
- checklist drafts
- risk labels
- first-pass decision candidates

### 2. Select Top Shots

Use scoring from N+3.18 and weekly review scoring from N+3.20:

- speed to ship
- buyer pain
- buyer access
- distribution leverage
- monetization clarity
- email-list fit
- content volume potential
- legal/TOS risk
- build complexity
- proof difficulty

Select a small batch for the week:

- 1 to 3 product or offer shots
- 1 to 2 lead magnet shots
- 5 to 20 content shots
- 1 distribution experiment

### 3. Build Draft Assets Locally

Local-only assets:

- product draft
- build pack
- content batch
- lead magnet draft
- listing draft
- launch checklist
- metrics intake template

No uploads, posting, selling, emailing, scraping, or account actions.

### 4. Manual Approval Gate

Before public or money-facing action, operator reviews:

- claims/proof
- fulfillment
- platform ToS
- refund/support boundary
- distribution plan
- email-list compliance
- risk review
- manual launch checklist

Approval phrases define review status only. They do not authorize automation unless a future prompt gives exact narrow permission.

### 5. Distribute Manually

Only after approval, the operator may manually:

- post content
- list a product
- send approved emails to opt-in lists
- participate in communities where allowed
- share a lead magnet
- record launch metrics

Ghoti remains local/artifact-only unless explicitly approved otherwise.

### 6. Record Metrics

Record manually:

- impressions
- views
- clicks
- opt-ins
- replies
- sales
- revenue
- refunds
- customer feedback
- traffic sources
- time spent

No scraping. No automatic platform login. No fake metrics.

### 7. Review

Run the future weekly review:

- summarize shots created
- summarize metrics
- identify winners
- identify stuck items
- identify distribution gaps
- identify email-list opportunities
- create decision queue candidates
- propose next 10 shots

### 8. Decide

Operator reviews decision candidates:

- `DOUBLE_DOWN`: push with more content/build effort
- `ITERATE`: change offer, hook, audience, or channel
- `PAUSE`: wait for missing data or capacity
- `KILL`: stop investing and salvage reusable parts
- `BUILD_NEXT`: build local MVP/draft
- `CREATE_CONTENT_BATCH`: draft content for exposure
- `CREATE_LEAD_MAGNET`: create owned-channel asset
- `REVIEW_LAUNCH_CHECKLIST`: prepare for manual launch review
- `COLLECT_MORE_DATA`: record facts before deciding

## Weekly Scoreboard

Track:

- shots created
- product drafts created
- build packs created
- content batches created
- distribution attempts
- email-list assets created
- manual launches
- opt-ins
- sales
- revenue
- killed ideas
- doubled-down ideas

The scoreboard should show bottlenecks:

- many ideas but no builds
- many builds but no distribution
- distribution without CTA
- CTA without email-list capture
- launches without metrics
- metrics without decisions

## Hard Boundaries

Never automate:

- posting
- selling
- outreach
- payments
- app-store actions
- scraping
- fake engagement
- fake proof
- account actions

Everything public, external, account-based, or money-facing remains human-approved and manually executed unless a future milestone explicitly narrows and approves a safe action.

## Cadence Verdict

Ghoti should become a weekly operating system for ethical volume: create, package, expose, measure, decide, repeat.
