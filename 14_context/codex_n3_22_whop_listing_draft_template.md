## Purpose

This file defines draft-only listing templates for Whop-style products, Gumroad-style products, and lead magnet landing pages. These are local copywriting artifacts only. They do not create accounts, upload files, set prices, collect payments, publish pages, or send emails.

## Whop-Style Listing Draft

```markdown

Status: draft_only / not_published / approval_required

Short, searchable, and specific.

## Subtitle

One sentence that names the buyer, problem, and outcome without exaggerated claims.

## Short Description

2 to 3 sentences explaining what the buyer gets and why it helps.

## Long Description

### The Problem

Describe the painful problem in buyer language.

### What You Get

- Deliverable 1:
- Deliverable 2:
- Deliverable 3:
- Bonus:

### How It Works

Explain how the buyer uses the product in 3 to 5 steps.

### Expected Result

Use careful language:
- "designed to help"
- "gives you a starting point"
- "helps organize"
- "can reduce setup friction"

Avoid guarantees:
- no guaranteed income
- no guaranteed grades
- no guaranteed followers
- no guaranteed business results

## Who It Is For

- Buyer type 1
- Buyer type 2

- People expecting guaranteed outcomes
```

## Verdict

These templates are safe for local drafting. They become risky only when connected to accounts, payments, publishing, email capture, or platform uploads, so those actions must remain separately approval-gated.
