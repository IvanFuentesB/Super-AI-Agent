# N+3.40 Giveaway And Raffle Safety Register

Status: Codex safety register only.
Date: 2026-05-05

## Idea Captured

The operator saw a video of someone winning giveaways through email-heavy or game-like raffle work. A possible idea was agent-owned email/accounts for raffle participation.

This idea is preserved, but automation is not approved.

## Risk Analysis

| Risk | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| ToS violations | Giveaway rules and platform policies often limit entries, identities, automation, and eligibility. |
| Spam | Automated email/account behavior can create spam-like patterns and reputation damage. |
| Fake accounts | Creating accounts to multiply entries can be deceptive and platform-banned. |
| Fraud | Misrepresenting identity, eligibility, or entry count can become legal risk. |
| Privacy | Giveaways can collect personal data and sell/share it with advertisers. |
| Account bans | Platforms can ban accounts for automation, bulk behavior, or suspicious entries. |
| Legal/regulatory concerns | Sweepstakes and contests are regulated; rules, odds, no-purchase requirements, and eligibility matter. |
| Scam exposure | Many giveaway notifications are scams designed to harvest data or payments. |

## Source-Check Notes

- FTC consumer guidance says real sweepstakes are free and by chance, and warns that contest promoters may sell entrant information.
- FTC guidance on endorsements notes that sweepstakes/contest incentives should be disclosed clearly in social media contexts.
- YouTube prohibits artificial engagement.
- TikTok integrity policies call out automation used to register or operate accounts in bulk, high-volume commercial abuse, and artificial engagement.

## Allowed Safe Version

Allowed only as local research/manual tracking:

- Research official giveaway rules.
- Track legitimate giveaways manually.
- Use one real identity/account only where allowed.
- Keep a local checklist of eligibility, odds, prize value, deadline, and data requested.
- Human review before entry.
- Record no-purchase requirements and privacy risks.
- Avoid any giveaway that asks for payment to win.

## Forbidden Without Explicit Legal/ToS Approval

- Fake accounts.
- Multiple identities.
- Mass entries.
- Botting.
- Captcha bypass.
- Auth/session bypass.
- Email automation to enter contests.
- Scripts that submit forms.
- Account creation at scale.
- Buying, selling, or trading entries.
- Engagement farming.
- Misleading contest posts.

## Verdict

Giveaway/raffle automation is research-only and likely reject for automation unless a specific workflow is proven legal, ToS-compliant, non-deceptive, and manually approved.

Ghoti may help build a local tracker/checklist for legitimate giveaways later. Ghoti must not enter giveaways, create accounts, send emails, or automate raffle participation.
