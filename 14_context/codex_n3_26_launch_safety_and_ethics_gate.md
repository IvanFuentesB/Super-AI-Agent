## Purpose

The launch safety gate defines what must stay blocked before any product, content, email, listing, or distribution action goes public. Ghoti can prepare artifacts, but the operator must approve and perform external actions manually unless a future milestone explicitly approves a narrow automation.

## Anti-Spam Rules

Not allowed:

- unsolicited mass outreach
- scraped contact lists
- purchased email lists
- automated DMs
- follow/unfollow automation
- comment spam
- link drops in communities
- fake engagement pods
- bot likes, views, comments, or followers

Allowed:

- draft outreach review templates
- draft helpful posts
- draft community-safe messages
- draft email content for opt-in lists
- document platform rules

## No Fake Proof

Not allowed:

- fake testimonials
- fabricated screenshots
- fake revenue screenshots
- fake user counts
- fake case studies
- invented customer results
- "as seen on" claims without proof

Required:

- label drafts clearly
- separate proof available from proof missing
- soften unproven claims
- remove claims that cannot be supported

## No Misleading Claims

Avoid:

- guaranteed income
- guaranteed followers
- guaranteed grades
- guaranteed business results
- medical/financial/legal promises
- exaggerated automation claims
- "zero work" or "instant money" framing

Prefer:

- "designed to help"
- "template for"
- "starting point"
- "checklist for"
- "can reduce setup friction"
- "helps organize"

## ToS / Legal Review

Review before public action:

- marketplace terms
- payment platform terms
- social platform rules
- email compliance requirements
- privacy/cookie requirements
- copyright/IP rights
- affiliate disclosure needs
- refund policy
- tax/business obligations where relevant

High-risk categories:

- finance/investing
- health/fitness
- legal/tax
- employment/academic outcomes
- app-store monetization
- AI automation involving accounts

## Refund And Support Boundaries

Every launch candidate should state:

- refund policy draft
- support channel
- response expectation
- what support includes
- what support excludes
- update promise
- version scope

Do not promise unlimited support for a low-priced product unless explicitly intended.

## Human Approval Gates

Explicit approval required before:

- publishing a listing
- uploading product files
- setting price
- activating payment
- sending email
- posting content
- outreach
- using live account credentials
- collecting customer data
- app-store submission
- affiliate/referral setup
- paid ads

## Approval Phrase Boundary

`APPROVE MANUAL PRODUCT LAUNCH REVIEW FOR <product_id>` means:

- launch pack reviewed
- operator may manually act externally
- Ghoti may record the review
- Ghoti still may not launch automatically

It does not mean:

- publish automatically
- upload automatically
- send emails
- scrape metrics
- charge customers
- change prices
- use accounts

## Ethics Gate Verdict

The safety gate keeps Ghoti pointed at ethical money-making: useful products, honest claims, consent-based audiences, and manual human control over public actions.
