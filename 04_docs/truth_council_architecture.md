# Truth Council Architecture

## Goal

Adversarial truth-seeking, not simple voting.

## Roles

- proposer
- challenger
- evidence collector
- adjudicator

## Outputs

- lead answer
- dissent
- consensus level
- confidence score
- evidence quality notes

## Warnings

- naive majority vote is not enough
- weak evidence plus agreement can still be wrong
- collusion risk matters if roles are not kept distinct
- dissent should be preserved instead of flattened away
