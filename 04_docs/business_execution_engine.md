# Business Execution Engine

## Legitimate Workflow Types

This system should support legitimate workflows such as:

- offer testing
- product, docs, and report generation
- owned-account content workflows
- research-to-experiment planning
- video-to-runbook conversion
- LinkedIn and CV asset maintenance
- GitHub and project organization

## Why Approval Gates Matter

Approval gates keep external actions, sensitive changes, and business-facing outputs inspectable before they become irreversible.

## Why Memory And Handoff Matter

Compact memory and handoff files reduce chat drift, preserve decisions, and make long-running work easier to resume without depending on one long session.

## Out Of Scope

This system should stay out of fake account creation, bulk email account creation, platform abuse, spam automation, CAPTCHA bypass, deceptive identity automation, and abusive multi-account behavior.
