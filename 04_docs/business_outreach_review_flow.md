# Business Outreach Review Flow

## Purpose

This is the first honest Ghoti scaffolding for local business outreach. It is planning-only and review-first.

## Current Phase

Ghoti can help structure the workflow, drafts, and review queue. Ghoti cannot send outreach, negotiate, spend money, or make commitments autonomously.

## Narrow Workflow

1. Identify candidate businesses with no site, a weak site, or an outdated site.
2. Draft a candidate offer or improvement angle.
3. Draft outreach copy for human review.
4. Queue the draft and notes for supervisor review.
5. Require explicit approval before any send, spend, or negotiation step.

## Required Gates

- no automatic sending
- no automatic money spend
- no automatic negotiation or pricing commitments
- no external contact without human approval
- no deceptive identity automation

## Later Explicit Path

- browser/operator path for business discovery
- compact memory summaries for leads and decisions
- finance/risk gate review for pricing, spend, and commitments
- owned-account send path only after explicit approval wiring exists
